Special Egg-Fry

Some tips for perfect boiled eggs

Do not use eggs directly from the refrigerator. Leave them out to reach room temperature. Start cracking the shells only after the eggs have fully cooled down. And finally, when it is time to slice the eggs, don’t use a knife. Use a thread, hold the ends between the thumb and index finger on each hand, and slice with it. You’ll get a clean cut.
